# Photo guide

Put your images in:

`public/images/order/`

Use exactly these filenames:

- `beef.jpg`
- `chicken-skewer.jpg`
- `vegan.jpg`

## Recommended image style

To make the design look clean:

- use vertical food photos
- keep the food centered
- avoid tiny food inside a big empty background
- use bright, sharp photos with strong contrast
- recommended ratio: around `4:5`
- recommended minimum size: `1200 x 1500`

## Best result tip

All three photos should have a similar look:

- same lighting style
- similar zoom level
- similar background feeling

That makes the page look much more professional.
